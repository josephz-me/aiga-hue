About

How happy are designers?

This is the basic question at the heart of the AIGA & Hue project. Using data from the 2019 AIGA Census, we developed a tool that would provide a fun and informative way to interact with physicalized data about the various satisfaction levels across design disciplines.

Stefanie Suk
Langston Wells
Joseph Zhang

Our hope is that this tool will help young professionals and college students understand more about what career path they might be interested in by providing an understanding of the satisfaction level and portfolios of designers across various industries.
